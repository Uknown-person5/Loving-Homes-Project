About Us


Learn more about our team and our mission to provide safe, caring, and comfortable services for every pet

Our Story & Passion

Founded with a singular vision to redefine pet care in Hong Kong, Loving Homes is more than just a boarding facility; it is a sanctuary designed by dog lovers, for dog lovers. We understand that your canine companion is a cherished member of your family, and leaving them behind—even for a short time—can be a stressful experience. That is why we have dedicated ourselves to creating a 'home away from home' environment that prioritizes safety, emotional well-being, and physical stimulation. Our state-of-the-art facility features climate-controlled indoor play barns, expansive private grass meadows, and dedicated agility courses, ensuring that every guest receives the exercise and social interaction they crave.

Our Commitment to Excellence

At Loving Homes, we set the gold standard for professional pet services. Our team consists of highly trained animal behaviorists and certified grooming specialists who provide personalized attention to every dog, regardless of breed or temperament. From our Premium Boarding packages that offer one-on-one photo updates and bespoke treat menus, to our energetic Daycare programs designed to build confidence and social skills, we ensure a transparent and loving experience. We pride ourselves on our meticulous safety protocols and our commitment to hygiene, giving you total peace of mind while you are away. Join the Loving Homes family today and discover a world where your pet’s happiness is our primary mission.
